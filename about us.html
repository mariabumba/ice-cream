About us

Our Story

Our story began when Loly inherited an old family recipe for a secret ice cream base. This recipe had been passed down through generations, and Loly knew she had something truly special in her hands. With the encouragement of Joel, her childhood friend and business partner, they set out to bring their ice cream to the world.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to create delightful ice cream experiences that bring joy to our customers. We strive to use the finest ingredients and innovative flavors to create unique and memorable taste sensations. Our vision is to become the go-to destination for ice cream lovers worldwide.

Team

We have a dedicated team of passionate ice cream enthusiasts who are committed to crafting the finest quality ice cream. From our expert artisans who create the flavors to our friendly staff who serve our customers, every member of our team plays a vital role in delivering exceptional ice cream experiences.
